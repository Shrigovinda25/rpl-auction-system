Tournament Format & Rules

League Format

  • Round Robin between Team A, B, and C.
  • Win: 2 points | Loss: 0 points
  • 1st Place: Direct Final 🏆
  • 2nd vs 3rd: Semi-Final -> Winner to Final

Badminton Rules

  • Format: Best of 5 Matches. First to 3 wins ties.
  • Matches: Boys Singles, Girls Singles, Boys Doubles, Girls Doubles, Mixed Doubles.
  • Players: Min 4-6 players. One player -> 1 Singles + 1 Doubles only.

Volleyball Rules

  • League/Semi: 15 points (single set)
  • Final: 25 points
  • Players: 6 Playing. Squad: 7-9 players.

Cricket Rules

  • League: 8 overs
  • Semi-Final / Final: 10 overs
  • Players: Playing XI -> 11 players. Squad: 11-14.
