Official Rules

RPL Tournament 2026 Guidelines

League Format

  • Round Robin between Team A, B, and C.
  • WIN: 2 points | LOSS: 0 points
  • 1st PLACE: Direct Final entry 🏆
  • 2nd vs 3rd: Semi-Final Match. Winner advances to the Final.
  • In case of point ties, Net Run Rate (NRR) or Sport Ranking will determine placement.

Badminton Category Rules

  • Best of 5 Format: First team to win 3 matches wins the overall tie.
  • Categories: Boys Singles, Girls Singles, Boys Doubles, Girls Doubles, Mixed Doubles.
  • Limit: One player can participate in max 1 Singles and 1 Doubles match.
  • Players must be members of the original squad reported to the admin.

Volleyball Match Rules

  • League & Semi: 15 points target (Single Set).
  • Final: 25 points target (Single Set).
  • Technicality: Both teams must rotate based on serving order.
  • Rotation faults will result in points awarded to the opponent.

Cricket Over Rules

  • League Games: 8 overs per inning.
  • Post-League: Semi-Finals & Finals will be 10 overs.
  • Powerplay: 2 Overs (League) / 3 Overs (Finals). Only 2 players allowed outside 30-yard circle.
  • Fair play rules apply. Umpires' decision is final.
